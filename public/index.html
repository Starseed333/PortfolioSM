Iron Meals - Full Stack

If your weeks start out with good intentions and good healthy meals but quickly devolve into fast food, convenience store food or greasy takeout food, then you could likely benefit from Iron-Meals. The application involves planning your meals with a convenient grocery shopping list generated by your recipe's added to your week. Once you generate a user profile with your personal information and what it is that you expect out of that week in regards to protein, fat and carb intakes then in no time will you be back on track. With consumers and families who are busier than ever, meal prepping simply makes it easier to eat well throughout the week.

Rock * Paper * Scissors

Each time its the opponents turn the player is notified. All this data is then stored at the database. Of course, like every game there is a chat box where the two players can message each other. The players are also notified when their opponent has left. It is indeed a very fun game.

Eat Da Burger - Sequelize

A Node, Express, Handlebars, MySQL, Sequelize ORM method, burger app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. This time the user can add their name to their creation. If they feel like having "seconds" they click the button and this creates a new burger for them or someone else to enjoy. They can also create a burger and not "devour" it, but rather let someone else enjoy their creation. This application was created using the MVC software architecture.
